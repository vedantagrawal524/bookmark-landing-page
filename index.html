Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of our FAQs. If you have any other questions you’d like answered please feel free to email us.

What is Bookmark?

We offer a simple and powerful bookmarking feature that lets you save your favorite websites for easy access anytime. No more searching — just click and go.

How can I request a new browser?

While we recommend using our service on the latest version of your preferred browser, if you need a new browser, you can download it directly from its official website. We support all major browsers.

Is there a mobile app?

Yes! We offer a mobile-friendly experience, and our app is available for both iOS and Android devices. Enjoy all the same features on the go, right from your phone or tablet.

What about other Chromium browsers?

We're currently optimized for Google Chrome and Opera. We're actively working on bringing full support to other Chromium-based browsers like Microsoft Edge and Brave very soon.
